Worldwide Wines is your premier East Coast winery!

Enjoy a taste of wine country right here in the heart of the city. At Worldwide Wines, we import the finest wines from around the world and bring them to our studio and store for you to sample and bring home.

Come by the studio anytime and let our knowledge staff help you pick the perfect wine for the occasion.The advice is great and the samples are always free.

We purchase in bulk from our partners around the world and we’re able to pass the benefit of those relationships on to our valued customers.

For those seeking a hand-on approach, we offer tours of a number east coast wineries with whom we partner. They take place the first friday each month.
